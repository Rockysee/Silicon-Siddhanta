# SECTION 2: VASTU PURUSHA MANDALA — THE GRID 🟢

**Structure:**
- Sacred geometric grid with **Brahma (ब्रह्मा)** at the **center (navel/nabhi)**
- Deities emanate outward from Brahma in concentric layers
- 8 directions + 1 center = 9 primary stations
- Each direction has specific characteristics, domains, and associated deities
- The grid is based on actual Earth directions (उत्तरी ध्रुव = North Pole; दक्षिणी ध्रुव = South Pole)

**Critical Rule:** True Earth direction is what matters — North is always North regardless of which way the house faces. House orientation doesn't change the actual directional energy.

**Brahma at Center:**
- Associated with **yellowness** (पीलापन), golden light, beginning of life
- Linked to: Bile secretion, cooking in womb, liver function (Jupiter/Brihaspati burns waste)
- Everything yellow = Brahma's domain (egg yolk, turmeric, Sun's golden rays)
- **This center must remain open/sacred — no heavy construction**

**Emanation Pattern:**
- Brahma first emanates **four primary deities**: Bhudhar, Arma, Vivahvaan, Mitra
- From these four, additional layers generate in **pairs (दो-दो)**
- These pairs create natural **gates (गेट्स)** 
- The entire structure follows geometric principles

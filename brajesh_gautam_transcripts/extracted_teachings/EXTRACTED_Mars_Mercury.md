## MARS (MANGAL) - EPISODE 13

### Foundational Philosophy of Mars

**Mars as Purusharth (Masculine Action Energy)**

Mars represents **Purusharth** – the principle of self-effort, action, and masculine initiative. A man must have intrinsic energy and capability; otherwise, life becomes meaningless. The teaching references Arjuna and Krishna, where Krishna instructs: "Do your Purusharth (engage in action)." One must not rely on others but on one's own strength.

**Mars as Nabhicenk (Navel Center)**

The Navel is Mars' seat in the body:
- It is the hub of all life energy and spiritual power (Pran Vayu operates from here)
- The navel controls five types of Vayu (air currents): Pran, Apan, Udaan, Vyan
- It is the body's "core" - like a fortress that holds one's strength
- When the navel is displaced (physically or energetically), all diseases follow
- The phrase "Mangal Bhavan Amangal Hari" means if your belly is sound, all diseases disappear

**Mars and the Elements**

- Mars is **Agni tattva** (fire element) – 72% fire at the navel center
- 12% water, 16% earth (approximate distribution)
- Fire burns, transforms, and purifies
- The digestive fire (Jatharani) is Mars-ruled
- Mars cannot mix with water (like fire and water don't combine) – hence Mars is exalted in Capricorn, debilitated in Cancer (water sign)

**Mars as Agni (Digestive Fire)**

- Controls digestion and immunity
- Represents willpower and initiative
- A strong Mars creates health, vitality, and energy
- A weak Mars leads to digestive issues, weakness, and dependency on others

### Mangal Dosh (Kuja Dosh)

**Definition:**
Mars in the 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th house creates Mangal Dosh (also called Kuja Dosh).

**Astrological Perspective:**
- Mars is a malefic planet (malefic = harmful influence)
- When placed in specific houses, it creates "defect" (Dosh)
- Believed to cause marital discord and compatibility issues

**Effects:**
- Marital tension and friction (especially 7th house)
- Loss of romance in relationships
- Aggressive behavior toward spouse
- If both partners have Mangal Dosh: effects cancel out (mutual compatibility)

**Significance:**
- Traditional belief: cannot marry someone without matching Dosh
- However, Brajesh suggests: Mars just needs understanding and proper channeling
- The "dosh" (defect) is actually untapped energy

### Remedies for Mars (Upay)

**Gemstone Remedy:**
- **Stone:** Moonga (Red Coral)
- **Metal:** Red/copper ring
- **Finger:** Ring finger (Anamika)
- **Day to Wear:** Tuesday (Mangalvar) early morning
- **Weight:** 5-8 Ratti (avoid 10, 15 Ratti – causes overdose)
- **Quality:** Italian Moonga is best; avoid cracked, black-spotted, or bent stones
- **Cost:** Prices vary by quality alone

**Key Principle:**
- Don't overstrain Mars (like not overfilling a pot)
- Small dose is better; increase gradually if needed

**Behavioral Remedies:**

1. **Develop Friendship (Maitrata):**
   - Cultivate friendships with males (specifically)
   - Be a true friend to others
   - Friendship is Mars's strength; isolation weakens it
   - The Navel (Nabhi) gains strength through companionship

2. **Feed Sweet Items to Children:**
   - Distribute sweets to young children
   - This strengthens Mars
   - Give chocolates, toffees, halwa to kids
   - If no children around, find elderly or other children to feed

3. **Blood Donation:**
   - Excellent Mars remedy
   - Donate blood regularly (check hemoglobin levels)
   - Mars rules blood, so giving blood strengthens this natural function
   - Highly beneficial, especially if Mars is afflicted

4. **Confidence and Self-Reliance:**
   - Develop Purusharth (self-effort)
   - Stop seeking blessings and do your own work
   - Be like Arjuna (self-reliant) not like Duryodhana (dependent on others)
   - Sun gives power to those who work independently

5. **Active Lifestyle:**
   - Exercise and strength training
   - Keep body fit and muscular
   - Maintain proper posture
   - Avoid lethargy and laziness

### Unique Insights and Philosophy

**Mars as Sycamore/Twin Brother of Earth:**
- Traditionally called Earth's "twin brother" (Dharti Ka Twin Brother)
- Has no water, no life (unlike Earth)
- Yet provides minerals: gold, aluminum, copper when processed
- Mars shapes raw material into form (like fire shapes metals)
- This is Mars's role: to shape and transform life

**Mars and Pharmacy Industry:**
- Mars rules all pharmaceutical and medicinal fields
- The entire pharmaceutical lobby has Mars power
- 2nd highest profit industry after war is pharmaceuticals
- Mars creates doctors and medical practitioners

**Debt and Mars (12th House Association):**
- If Mars's drishti falls on 12th house: inevitable debt
- But don't fear debt; use it to create
- Small debts teach lessons; don't repeat mistakes after learning
- Confidence is key: "I will pay back and move forward"
- Mars gives ability to earn and repay
- God provides the means; don't sit idle

**Mars and Workplace Success:**
- Mars in 10th with Saturn: excellent business partnerships
- Can become property dealer, manufacturer, military officer
- Mars+Jupiter combination: ideal for military and police
- Mars's drishti on 10th: gives commanding position

**The Dance of Mars and Saturn:**
- Mars (fire/aggression) + Saturn (restraint/limitation) = perfect balance
- Saturn provides foundation; Mars provides action
- When Mars enters Saturn's house: destroys restraint (burns fuel)
- When Saturn enters Mars's house: restrains action (fuel stored)
- Combined: Mars gives fuel, Saturn organizes use of fuel

## KEY TEACHING METHODOLOGIES USED BY BRAJESH GAUTAM

1. **Storytelling:** Uses anecdotes and mythological references (Arjuna, Hanuman, Durga) to illustrate concepts

2. **Comparative Analysis:** Explains planets in relation to other grahas (planets), elements, and principles

3. **Body-Centered Teaching:** Connects planets to specific body parts and health issues for practical understanding

4. **Behavioral Remedy Emphasis:** Focuses on action and lifestyle changes over mere gemstone remedies

5. **Philosophical Depth:** Integrates Vedic philosophy, Tantra, and practical astrology

6. **Practical Examples:** Uses modern examples (Ramdev, Trump, Elon Musk) to illustrate principles

7. **Gender-Conscious Teaching:** Acknowledges different manifestations for males and females

8. **Integration with Religion:** Shows how planetary principles manifest in religious/spiritual practices

## OVERALL FRAMEWORK

Both Mars and Mercury operate within Brajesh's larger Jyotish framework that emphasizes:

1. **Self-Effort Over Dependence:** Purusharth is primary; don't rely on blessings alone
2. **Health as Foundation:** Physical health (through proper Mars/Budh functioning) underlies all success
3. **Dharmic Living:** Using planetary energies for righteous living (dharma)
4. **Service and Community:** Using one's strengths (Mars or Mercury) to serve society
5. **Integration of Opposites:** Mars (action) + Mercury (wisdom) = balanced living

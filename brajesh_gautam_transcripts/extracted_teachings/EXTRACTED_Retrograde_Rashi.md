## RETROGRADE PLANETS (VAKRI GRAH)

### Definition and Core Concept of Vakri

**What Vakri Means:**
- **Vakri** (वक्री) literally means "retrograde" or "backward-moving" in the astrological framework
- It is not that a planet actually moves backward in space; rather, it appears to move backward from Earth's perspective due to the Sun's gravitational pull
- Vakri is a phenomenon of **relative motion and perception** - when viewed from Earth, the planet appears to be moving in the reverse direction

**The Fundamental Principle:**
When a planet becomes vakri, it operates through a backward/reversed lens in an individual's life:
- **Vakri planets pull your life backward in time**
- They make you focus on past events, past relationships, past mistakes, and past patterns
- They create a psychological state where you are "moving backward" even while life continues forward
- This is why the person feels stuck, delayed, or constantly revisiting old issues

### How Vakri Planets Function

**The Backward Pull (Peeche Khintchna):**
- Vakri planets literally drag your consciousness and life circumstances **backward** (पीछे की ओर)
- When a planet is vakri in your natal chart (birth moment), it activates this backward-pulling property for your entire life
- The planet doesn't need to be currently retrograde in the sky to affect you - **if you were born when it was vakri, you carry this impact**

**Example from Teaching:**
Brajesh gives the analogy of two people running:
- If one person (Moona) is running forward at normal speed and another person (Minall) is also running forward but at a slower speed
- Minall looks like she is moving backward relative to Moona
- Minall is actually moving forward but **feels and perceives herself as moving backward**
- This is exactly what vakri planets do - they create a perception of backward movement even though life moves forward

**The Power of Vakri Planets:**
- Vakri planets are considered **highly powerful** (बहुत पावरफुल)
- They have a unique capacity: when they awaken their power, they can reverse the flow of events
- A person with a well-developed vakri planet can have a **Vak Siddhi** - the power of speech/words where their statements manifest into reality
- This is similar to how saints and accomplished yogis can accomplish miracles through speech

### Individual Vakri Planet Effects

**Planets That Can Be Vakri:**
- **Mercury (Budh)** - vakri
- **Mars (Mangal)** - vakri
- **Jupiter (Brihaspati)** - vakri
- **Venus (Shukra)** - vakri
- **Saturn (Shani)** - vakri
- **Rahu & Ketu** - permanently vakri (स्थायी वक्री)
- **Moon (Chandra)** - NEVER vakri
- **Sun (Surya)** - NEVER vakri

**Why Sun and Moon Don't Go Vakri:**
- The Sun and Moon are the central forces of the cosmos
- They don't become vakri from Earth's perspective
- However, if we consider geocentric perspective, the Sun and Moon appear to move normally through the zodiac

#### Mars Vakri (Mangal Vakri)

**Effect on Life:**
- When Mars is vakri in the 4th house (house of home, mother, property, friendships, property inheritance)
- The person goes "back in time" regarding these areas
- You find yourself constantly thinking about your mother, your home, your friends, past property situations
- The person is pulled back into old family dynamics and past grievances
- **Forward progress in matters of home, family, and property is blocked**

**Characteristics:**
- Mars rules action and courage; when vakri, actions become reversed or blocked
- People with Mars vakri in the 4th house often revisit family traumas and unresolved relationships

#### Saturn Vakri (Shani Vakri)

**Effects:**
- Saturn vakri creates extreme blockages and delays
- It obscures things from view - Saturn is darkness (अंधकार), and it puts a veil over your destiny
- Saturn vakri can be "भयंकर" (bhayankara - terrible/fearsome)
- Even doctors and astrologers cannot detect the problems because Saturn puts a veil
- **Saturn creates a purdah (curtain) over the person's fortune such that even astrology cannot see it**

**Medical Implications:**
- Cancer or serious diseases may not be detected until very advanced stages
- Saturn vakri can block diagnoses and hide illnesses
- Doctors themselves cannot detect problems that Saturn has concealed

**Solutions:**
- Serve servants/service workers (sewa of the humble)
- This improves Saturn's condition over time
- Use oil on Thursday for Saturn improvement (if 9th house permits)
- **Avoid worshipping Saturn or Shiva when Saturn is weak/damaged** - this can worsen the situation and destroy knees and mobility

#### Jupiter Vakri (Brihaspati Vakri)

**Effects:**
- Jupiter represents knowledge, wisdom, and expansion
- When vakri, knowledge becomes reversed or blocked
- The person cannot expand or grow properly
- Luck and fortunate circumstances are delayed or blocked

#### Mercury Vakri (Budh Vakri)

**Effects:**
- Mercury vakri creates communication blockages
- Business and trade suffer
- If someone has a mercury vakri dosh (defect), they may have speech impediments or communication difficulties
- Mercury vakri can cause sudden business failures

**Special Note:**
- There are high chances that if Mercury is vakri, there may be "budh dosh" (mercury defect)
- This may not be visible in other houses where Mercury stands

#### Venus Vakri (Shukra Vakri)

**Effects:**
- Relationship matters become complex
- Attraction and love become reversed or problematic
- Material pleasures and beauty become inaccessible

## TIMING AND PHASES OF VAKRI

### When Planets Become Vakri

**Causation:**
- Planets become retrograde/vakri due to **the Sun's gravitational pull** (सूरज के आकर्षण से)
- This is a natural cosmic phenomenon that repeats in cycles
- The duration varies for each planet

**Universal Law (Brahmanical Natural Law):**
A universal law governs vakri planets:
- **When a planet is vakri in space and you were born during that retrograde period, the vakri condition becomes your life pattern**
- The planet will **continue to create vakri effects in your life even when it is not retrograde in current time**
- This is because at your birth moment, the planet imprinted this pattern into your being

### Duration of Vakri Influence

**Key Teaching - Vakri Effect Reversal:**
When a retrograde planet becomes direct (moves forward again in space):
- It takes **2 months to reverse its effect**
- After this transition period, the planet's influence changes
- But the natal condition (if you were born with it vakri) still remains

**Example:**
- If Mercury was retrograde when you were born, the vakri effect is permanent in your chart
- If a planet **becomes retrograde after your birth**, its effects can be reversed when it goes direct again
- Birth moment vakri = permanent life pattern
- Post-birth vakri = temporary effect (2 months to reverse)

## REMEDIES FOR VAKRI PLANETS

### General Principle of Remedies

**The Core Rule:**
- **Upay/Remedy works only if your chart allows it** (कुंडली अलाउ करे तो)
- You cannot do any remedy without the chart's permission
- Even if you follow all remedies perfectly, if your prarabdh (destiny) doesn't allow, results won't come

**Why Some Remedies Fail:**
- Many people follow remedies religiously but see no results
- This is because their chart doesn't permit that particular remedy
- A specialist astrologer (like a doctor) knows which remedies suit which person
- You cannot self-prescribe remedies without proper analysis

### Specific Remedies

#### For Vakri Planets Generally

**Remedial Approach:**
- The main principle: **if a planet is vakri, then work to improve its condition**
- This can develop the planet's positive power (Vak Siddhi)
- With proper work, a vakri planet can become a source of extraordinary power

**Why Remedies Sometimes Don't Work:**
- If you wear a gemstone for a vakri planet and your chart doesn't permit it
- The stone won't suit you and can actually harm you
- Stones (Ratna) amplify the planet's effects - if the chart doesn't permit amplification, you get negative results

#### Chakra/Energy Body Remedies

**Cord Cutting (Nadi/Dori Cutting):**
- A mental/energetic practice to sever unhelpful connections
- If certain people (mother, father, aunt, uncle) are creating problems in your life, you can do mental cord-cutting
- This breaks the energy connection but requires **mental stability and higher chakra work**

**How to Perform Cord Cutting:**
1. Sit in isolation with good insulation from the earth (wooden chair or cushion)
2. Close your eyes and create a mental room with two chairs
3. Visualize the other person sitting across from you
4. Imagine a cord connecting your navel (nabhi) to their navel (this is DNA connection)
5. Take mental scissors and cut the cord at both ends
6. Place the cut cord in a blue bag with a seal and mentally throw it into the cosmos
7. Thank the person and return them to their body, then exit the visualization

**Important Warning:**
- This is not a casual practice
- If you cut a cord while feeling emotional pain, the cutting becomes "dead/useless"
- You must be in a higher/upper chakra state (calm, meditative) for it to work
- If you cut while in pain, the cord will multiply instead of being severed
- This requires deep understanding - it can be misused

#### Saturn Remedies

**For Saturn Dosh:**
- **Serve servants/humble people** - this is the primary remedy
- This improves Saturn's power and removes blockages
- Service workers (sewa) must be served with respect

**Meditation/Energy Work:**
- For Saturn issues in the lower body (legs, knees), focus on service
- Shaman's water (cremation ground water) kept in the home can help
- **Warning:** Do NOT worship Saturn or Shiva when Saturn is weak - this can destroy your knees

#### General Principle - Offering/Sacrifice

**The Universal Principle (Bali/Balidan):**
- Every planet (especially daitya/demon planets like Saturn, Rahu, Ketu) requires **bali** (sacrifice/offering)
- This means you must give something up or sacrifice something in that area
- The planets demand this payment
- When you have sudden good fortune, prepare for corresponding loss (to balance)

**The Law of Balance:**
- If you gain something positive, the planet will demand bali
- Big house = possible loss through accident or repair costs
- New car = possible accident or damage
- Good news = prepare for corresponding bad news
- This is how the cosmic balance maintains itself

## RASHIS AND PLANET BEHAVIOR IN SIGNS

### Understanding Rashi (Zodiac Signs)

**What Are Rashis:**
- Rashis are the 12 zodiac signs positioned in a 360-degree circle
- Each rashi has specific characteristics and animal/symbol associations
- The rashi determines the **nature and behavior of planets placed in it**
- Your natal chart shows which planets are in which rashis
- This determines your base personality and how each area of life unfolds

**Core Teaching:**
- The rashi itself is fixed and unchanging
- It gives basic character to any planet placed in it
- You may have "mixed" influences from multiple planets, but broadly, each rashi imparts its quality

### The Twelve Rashis and Their Characteristics

#### 1. Aries (Mesha/मेष)

**Symbol:** Ram (Meda)
**Ruling Planet:** Mars (Mangal)
**Nature:** 
- Aggressive, headstrong, always pushing forward
- Constantly wants to butt heads and assert itself
- Very powerful and warrior-like
- Moves with force and courage
- Cannot be stopped once it decides something
- Must lead and be at the front

**Positive Qualities:** Courage, initiative, pioneering spirit
**Negative Qualities:** Aggression, stubbornness, inability to compromise
**Key Trait:** Must give its head/opinion on everything; if not listened to, becomes frustrated

#### 2. Taurus (Vrishabha/वृषभ)

**Symbol:** Bull/Buffalo
**Ruling Planet:** Venus (Shukra)
**Nature:**
- Once it settles into something, it becomes immovable
- Extremely stubborn - "locked in place"
- Will not budge or change position
- Very stable and steady
- Cannot be shaken from its convictions

**Positive Qualities:** Stability, loyalty, persistence, groundedness
**Negative Qualities:** Stubbornness, inflexibility, resistance to change
**Key Trait:** "Set it and forget it" - once decided, no discussion possible

#### 3. Gemini (Mithun/मिथुन)

**Symbol:** Twins
**Ruling Planet:** Mercury (Budh)
**Nature:**
- Dual nature (Dwi-swabhav - द्वि-स्वभाव)
- "Yes and no" simultaneously
- Ambivalent about everything
- Will take things along or leave things behind
- Indecisive but adaptable
- Playful and full of tricks (athkheliyan)

**Positive Qualities:** Flexibility, communication, versatility, intelligence
**Negative Qualities:** Inconsistency, indecision, superficiality
**Key Trait:** Dual approach to everything - two paths at once

#### 4. Cancer (Karka/कर्क)

**Symbol:** Crab
**Ruling Planet:** Moon (Chandra)
**Nature:**
- Caring and nurturing (caring nature)
- Holds onto things tightly - won't let go
- Emotional and sensitive
- Protective of loved ones
- Can become suffocating in care if negative planets involved
- Family-focused

**Positive Qualities:** Nurturing, protective, emotional depth, family loyalty
**Negative Qualities:** Over-protectiveness, emotional manipulation, inability to let go
**Key Trait:** Can love so much that they strangle/suffocate the other person (like how crab clamps)

**Example:** Parents with Cancer influence may hover over children so much that the children never get freedom

#### 5. Leo (Simha/सिंह)

**Symbol:** Lion
**Ruling Planet:** Sun (Surya)
**Nature:**
- Royal, proud, majestic
- Wants to lead and dominate
- Won't listen to anyone's authority

**Positive Qualities:** Leadership, generosity, courage, confidence
**Negative Qualities:** Arrogance, pride, need for attention

**Behavior in Groups:** Enters the gathering and immediately commands attention; won't accept anyone else's leadership

#### 6. Virgo (Kanya/कन्या)

**Positive Qualities:** Analysis, attention to detail, service orientation, cleanliness

**Positive Qualities:** Justice, balance, diplomacy, fairness
**Negative Qualities:** Indecision, avoiding conflict unnecessarily, people-pleasing
**Key Trait:** Constantly works to balance opposing forces

#### 8. Scorpio (Vrishchika/वृश्चिक)

## CHARACTERISTICS OF RASHI TYPES

### Chara, Sthira, and Dvi-Swabhava Rashis

**Chara Rashis (Moving/Changeable):**
- Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn
- These are for **starting new work/construction**
- If you start construction in a chara lagna (changeable ascendant), the work will continue without stopping
- Once the first hammer strikes, momentum continues
- Good for beginning projects

**Sthira Rashis (Fixed/Stable):**
- Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius
- These are for **final completion and stability**
- Good for marriage ceremonies (wedding should be in Leo lagna - it will be strong and lasting)
- Used for Diwali worship dates and important ceremonies
- Once established, these remain solid

**Dvi-Swabhava Rashis (Dual Nature):**
- Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces
- These have both moving and fixed qualities
- Can go and come, move and settle
- Used for mixed purposes

### Practical Applications of Rashi Understanding

**Construction Timing:**
- Start new construction during a **moving (chara) lagna**
- Finish during a **fixed (sthira) lagna**
- If you start in a fixed lagna, work will stall and face delays

**Marriage Timing:**
- Marry during **Leo (Simha) or other fixed rashis**
- These ensure the marriage is strong and lasting
- Specific dates in Diwali celebrations use Leo lagna

**Life Adjustments:**
- If your ascendant (lagna) is in a particular rashi, you understand your core nature
- You can then understand how planets behave when placed in your chart
- This helps predict which areas of life will be easy and which will be challenging

## PLANET DIGNITY CONCEPTS

### Own Sign (Sva-Rashi/स्व-राशि)

**Concept:**
- Each planet has its own rashi where it is most powerful and comfortable
- When a planet is in its own sign, it expresses naturally and powerfully

**Planet Ownerships:**
- Aries (1) = Mars
- Taurus (2) = Venus (also Libra)
- Gemini (3) & Virgo (6) = Mercury
- Cancer (4) = Moon
- Leo (5) = Sun
- Libra (7) = Venus
- Scorpio (8) = Mars
- Sagittarius (9) = Jupiter
- Capricorn (10) = Saturn
- Aquarius (11) = Saturn
- Pisces (12) = Jupiter

**Effect:** Planet in own sign = most natural expression, strong results

### Exaltation (Uccha/उच्च)

**Concept:**
- Each planet has a specific sign where it is exalted - at its highest power
- Even higher than own sign - this is supreme power

**Key Exaltations:**
- Sun exalted in Aries
- Moon exalted in Taurus
- Mercury exalted in Virgo
- Mars exalted in Capricorn
- Jupiter exalted in Cancer
- Venus exalted in Pisces
- Saturn exalted in Libra
- Rahu & Ketu have exaltation-debilitation axes

**Effect:** Exalted planet = maximum power and beneficial results

### Debilitation (Neecha/नीच)

**Concept:**
- Opposite sign to exaltation - planet is weak and confused
- The planet cannot express properly here
- Results are blocked or negative

**Key Debilitations:**
- Sun debilitated in Libra
- Moon debilitated in Scorpio
- Mercury debilitated in Pisces
- Mars debilitated in Cancer
- Jupiter debilitated in Capricorn
- Venus debilitated in Virgo
- Saturn debilitated in Aries
- Rahu & Ketu opposite to their exaltations

**Effect:** Debilitated planet = weakness, obstacles, negative results in that area

### Fallen Planet (Pata-Gat)

**Concept:**
- A planet that falls from its position of strength
- Can occur through transits or other astrological factors
- Creates sudden reversals

## CHAKRA AND ENERGY BODY CONNECTIONS

### Planets and Chakras (Nadis)

**How Planets Map to Body:**
- Each planet rules specific energy centers (chakras) and energy channels (nadis)
- In the body, there are 72,000 nadi (energy channels)
- Main nadis: Ida (left), Pingala (right), Sushumna (center)
- Chakras: Energy vortexes that correspond to planets and their functions

**Solar Nadi (Surya Nadi - सूरज नाड़ी):**
- Related to Sun
- Controls consciousness and awareness
- Right channel (Pingala)

**Lunar Nadi (Chandra Nadi - चंद्र नाड़ी):**
- Related to Moon
- Controls emotions and receptivity
- Left channel (Ida)

**Mooladhara and Svadhisthana:**
- Chakras ruled by Saturn and Jupiter
- If these are blocked, money cannot come
- If these are blocked, you get caught in maya (illusion)
- No job or success manifests

**Heart Chakra (Anahata):**
- If blocked, the person cannot understand love, peace, or health
- The heart literally struggles to beat properly
- If you shut this chakra, you shut off emotions

**Third Eye (Ajna/Third Nadi):**
- Where Ida and Pingala terminate
- Where Sushumna continues upward
- This is where cosmic connection happens (Shiva's third eye)
- Contact with the universe comes from here

### Blocked Chakras and Life Effects

**When Chakras Close:**
- Blocked chakra = that area of life doesn't work
- Brain doesn't receive signals from that area
- The person has dullness in that area of life

**Examples of Blocked Chakras:**
- If money chakra is blocked: No money comes despite hard work
- If health chakra is blocked: Constant health issues
- If relationship chakra is blocked: No love manifests
- If heart chakra is blocked: No compassion, no understanding of emotions

## THE PHILOSOPHY OF RETROGRADE/VAKRI IN BRAJESH'S FRAMEWORK

**Time and Consciousness:**
- Vakri planets operate on the principle of **time reversal in consciousness**
- They make you live in the past while your body lives in the present

- The universe continues moving forward, but you feel stuck or moving backward

### Prarabdh and Retrograde Planets

**Prarabdh (Destiny) Concept:**
- Your chart represents your destiny (prarabdh) from past-life karmas
- 12 houses represent the 12 areas of destiny
- When a planet is vakri at birth, it becomes part of your prarabdh

**The Binding Nature:**
- You cannot change what's bound in your prarabdh easily
- Only through proper remedies, meditation, and energy work can you shift it
- Planets demand "bali" (sacrifice/offering) before releasing their grip
- Quick fixes don't work; only sustained practice works

### Development of Vak Siddhi Through Vakri Planets

**The Paradox of Vakri:**
- Vakri planets appear to be obstacles
- But if properly cultivated, they develop **Vak Siddhi** - the power of speech
- This is similar to how saints and accomplished beings have miraculous powers
- A person with awakened vakri planet can manifest things through speech
- Their words become reality - they become like a conduit of divine power

**The Work Required:**
- Developing vak siddhi from a vakri planet requires conscious work
- Meditation, pranayama, energy work, service, and spiritual practice
- It's not automatic - it requires intention and dedication
- Once developed, the person can accomplish extraordinary things

**Caution with Negative Planets:**
